Realm of the Undead - Controls

Console Key Description
upKey 'w' - Move Up
downKey 's' - Move Down
leftKey 'a' - Move Left
rightKey 'd' - Move Right
reloadKey 'r' - Reload
gun1Key '1' - Switch to Gun 1
gun2Key '2' - Switch to Gun 2
spawnZombieKey 'z' - Spawn Zombie
mouseButton Fire equipped weapon
interactKey 'f' - Interact
